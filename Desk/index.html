Q

Why is almost everything Apple?

A

Well not because I think PC / Androids are disgusting, it's because my family is tied to the ecosystem, like how am I suppose to make a Facetime call with an Android?

On the computer side I could have choose the PC (with Linux on it), but this comes down on how I choose operating systems. Windows has the best compatibility but bloated, Linux is the least bloated but for me it's too unstable, and finally macOS is the middle of those two (for me).
